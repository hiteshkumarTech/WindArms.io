## WINDARMS
"The War Above The Storm"

Imagine a civilization that built cities floating thousands of meters above Earth.

Below them...

An endless sea of storms.

Entire continents disappeared centuries ago.

The only remaining civilizations exist on floating megastructures held aloft by ancient wind engines.

Every battle takes place above an infinite hurricane.

## World Rules (Never Break)

WindArms is **NOT** fantasy.

WindArms is **NOT** magical.

WindArms is **NOT** superhero-based.

Every ability must be believable within the world's technology.

All powers originate from:

* Atmospheric pressure engineering
* Wind manipulation through advanced machinery
* Kinetic energy storage
* Storm energy harvesting
* Electromagnetic technology
* Mechanical engineering
* Aerodynamics
* Advanced materials

No spellcasting.
No glowing magical hands.
No elemental magic.
No summoned creatures.
No teleportation through unexplained means.

Every ability must feel engineered.

See [../gameplay/abilities.md](../gameplay/abilities.md) for the ability-design framework built on these rules, and [../gameplay/operators.md](../gameplay/operators.md) for how they're applied to the four founding operators.
